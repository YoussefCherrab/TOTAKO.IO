How to Play

Master the Diamond Challenge in minutes

Game Rules

1
Players take turns clicking on lines between diamond cells on the board.
2
When you complete all edges around a diamond cell, you capture it — your symbol appears inside.
3
If you capture a cell, you do NOT get an extra turn (unlike classic Dots & Boxes).
4
The player with the most captured cells when all edges are taken wins!

⏱ Turn Timer

Each player (and the AI) has 5 seconds per turn. If time runs out, the turn passes automatically to the next player — no random move is placed. Stay focused!

🤖 Gemini AI Opponent

The AI opponent is powered by Google's Gemini AI and thinks about the best strategic moves. The AI also has a 5-second timer — if it doesn't respond in time, its turn is skipped and you play again.

Strategy Tips

  • Avoid completing 3 edges of a cell — your opponent can finish it next turn.
  • Look for chains of cells where completing one leads to capturing many.
  • Control the corners and edges first to create favorable chain opportunities.
  • Sacrifice a few cells to break an opponent's chain.

About TOTAKO

A strategic diamond-grid game

What is TOTAKO?

TOTAKO — Diamond Challenge is a free, browser-based strategic board game inspired by the classic "Dots and Boxes" concept, reimagined on a unique diamond-shaped grid.

Play against a Gemini AI opponent or challenge 2–4 friends on the same device. The game is designed to be fast, fun, and strategically deep.

Features

  • 🤖 AI opponent powered by Google Gemini
  • 👥 Local multiplayer for 2–4 players
  • ⏱ 5-second turn timer for both player and AI
  • 📱 Mobile-friendly, installable as PWA
  • 🎮 No account needed — play instantly

Creator

TOTAKO was created by Youssef Cherrab as a passion project to bring strategic board gaming to the browser with a modern, clean interface.

Have feedback or ideas? Contact us!

Technology

Built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies beyond fonts and the Gemini AI API. Runs fully in your browser and can be installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Overview

TOTAKO ("we," "us," or "our") operates the TOTAKO Diamond Challenge website and game. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of our service.

Information We Collect

Game Data: TOTAKO stores your best scores locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

AI Moves: When you play against the Gemini AI, the current board state (an abstract grid description) is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate AI moves. No personal information is included in these requests.

No Account Required: We do not require registration, collect email addresses, or store any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini API: AI gameplay uses the Gemini API. Board state data (not personal data) is sent to Google's servers. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Google Fonts: We load fonts from Google's CDN. Google may collect usage data per their privacy policy.

Advertising: We may display advertisements. Third-party advertisers may use cookies to serve relevant ads. We do not control these cookies.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save your game scores. We do not use tracking cookies ourselves. Third-party advertisement networks may set their own cookies.

Children's Privacy

TOTAKO is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
